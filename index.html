N-Gram Overlap Example Selector

The December 2022 paper In-context Examples Selection for Machine Translation found improved performance for multi-shot prompts of large language models when the example prompts were chosen and arranged based on their N-Gram overlap score with the final task prompt. I implemented the selection algorithm detailed in the paper, and contributed it to LangChain:

https://github.com/langchain-ai/langchain/pull/812
